Find a Time

Create a poll and find the best time to meet with your group.

How it works:

  • Click and drag to mark when you are free for the week you select.
  • Save your availability so it is shared with your group.
  • Create or open a poll to propose a date range and meeting length.
  • Best Overlap ranks times that work for the most people and avoids Google Calendar conflicts.
  • Opt into multiple time slots. When the creator finalizes one, it is added to everyone who opted in.
